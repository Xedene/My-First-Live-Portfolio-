01 — about

Who I am

I'm a 21-year-old IT enthusiast from the Philippines. I do this because I genuinely love it — the challenge of finding what's hidden, understanding how systems break, and getting better every time.

I stopped college in 2025 and kept going anyway. Everything I know comes from hands-on practice, competitions, and a lot of time deliberately breaking things. I don't wait for a curriculum.

In 2024 I competed with Team Trojans at HACK4Gov Regional (DICT Region 1) and we took first place out of 12 teams. That same year I reported a valid vulnerability to Michelin through responsible disclosure and made their Hall of Fame.

Right now I'm focused on web vulnerability research and bug bounty. I look for XSS, IDOR, auth bypasses — the kind of bugs that actually matter in the real world.
